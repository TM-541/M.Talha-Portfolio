A. Project Overview

A highly secure, decentralized LoRa mesh networking system designed for critical environments. It integrates an AI-driven firewall and multi-layered cryptographic protocols to ensure resilient, tamper-proof communications without cellular dependency.

Encryption Flow

  • AES-128-CBC: Payload encryption

AI Firewall Architecture

Mesh Networking
